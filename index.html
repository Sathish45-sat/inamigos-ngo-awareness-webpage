About Our NGO

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla. Originating from Chhattisgarh, India, the foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through education, social welfare, environment conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, and skill development.

We maintain the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and development integration in our operations. Our certifications make it easier for donors to secure tax benefits while supporting impactful CSR initiatives.

  • Section 8 Registered NGO
  • 80G & 12A Certified
  • NITI Aayog Registered
  • CSR-1 Registered
  • IAF ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Provides food, clothing, and other essential survival support to underprivileged families and communities.

Impact: 50,000+ meals distributed

Project BACHPANSHALA

Supports underprivileged children by providing basic primary education, digital literacy, and core life skills training.

Impact: Basic education & literacy support

Project JEEV

Focused on stray animal welfare by ensuring regular food, shelter, rescue, and veterinary care through our volunteer network.

Impact: 50+ stray animals fed daily

Project UDAAN

Empowers women by collaborating with rural self-help groups, promoting financial independence, skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness.

Impact: Empowerment & hygiene awareness

Project PRAKRITI

Promotes environmental sustainability through tree plantation drives, seed sowing, and eco-friendly agricultural practices.

Impact: 20,000+ saplings planted

Project VIKAS

Provides internships and skill development opportunities across domains like research, digital marketing, content writing, data operations, and social work.

Impact: 30,000+ interns trained
